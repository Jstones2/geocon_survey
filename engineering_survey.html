Engineering Survey

Engineering surveying is a specialized branch of surveying that provides crucial data for the planning, design, and execution of construction projects. At Geocon Survey Ltd, we utilize advanced equipment and technology to ensure accurate topographic measurements, elevation profiles, and boundary outlines. These surveys play a vital role in infrastructure projects such as roads, bridges, buildings, and dams, ensuring safety, efficiency, and compliance with regulatory standards.

Our Success Story
