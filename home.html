Vision

To be a model of expertise, professionalism and delivery in Land and Engineering survey, GIS, Remote sensing and Planning across the country and beyond.

Mission

To give our clients solutions that are project tailored to suit their needs, with attention on quality product, professional management and sound advice.

Cadastral and Engineering Survey

Geocon Ltd offers planning, sub-division, amendment of RIMs, preparation of deed plans among others. Engineering Survey is a component in this division.

GIS and Mapping

We provide mapping services for our clients to collect and systematize location related data. From data acquisition, processing, analysis, and management, Geodev Kenya is able to provide comprehensive GIS & Mapping services.

Environmental and Social Impact Assessment and Audit

We aim at determining and presenting the environmental impacts of a project, identifying their form, and realistic mitigation measures for the negative aspects while maximizing the positive ones.

Physical Planning

Our physical planning methodology entails understanding the needs and aspirations of all the involved parties with the objective of solving urban and regional phenomena through providing sound practical solutions.

Aerial Photography

We supply clear and accurate high-resolution aerial photographs and satellite images to help our clients to visualize, capture and analyze spatial data present in the images. Our team ensures that image products supplied meet the required standards.

Resettlement Action Plan (RAP)

We prepare Resettlement Action Plans to guide and ensure proper resettlement and compensation of affected people and communities.
