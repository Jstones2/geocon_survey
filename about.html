About Geocon Survey Ltd

Engineering and Survey Experts

Geocon surveys limited is a private limited company incorporated in August 19, 2020 under companies Act, 2015, Business number PVT-5JU965K.

We are also registered with the Kenya Revenue Authority as PIN No. P051996014K.

We have highly skilled and professional executive and staff to provide world-class surveying, GIS, Planning and remote sensing services to private, corporate and government clients.

We focus on delivering results of the highest quality and accuracy to our clients within set time frame and budget.

We value the safety and well being of all our staff and clients. We have strong concern over sustainable development goals as far as environment protection is concerned.
